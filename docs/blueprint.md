# **App Name**: Prana Mithra

## Core Features:

- Voice Consultation: Speech-to-text (Telugu) input, text-to-speech responses for remote consultations. Simple queue and ASHA assignment.
- AI Symptom Checker: Voice/text based AI powered symptom checker augmented with question trees to triage for common conditions, providing home care recommendations or referral advice.
- Emergency Alert: Single-tap SOS sends GPS, voice note, and nearest clinic contact + ASHA. Auto-fallback to SMS/USSD if no internet.
- Maternal & Child Health Tracker: Timeline for pregnancies & vaccination schedule with voice reminders and appointment prompts (ASHA tasks).
- Traditional Knowledge Collection: Audio/video uploads (elder interviews) stored with metadata, language, and permissions. Initially read-only.
- ASHA Dashboard: ASHA worker dashboard for case lists, appointment management, and offline data capture.
- Pharmacy Availability Tracker: SMS/WhatsApp/USSD-based stock updates for small shops + app list for users.
- Healthcare facility locator: Healthcare facility locator with real-time bed availability and specialist info
- Health scheme awareness module: Health scheme awareness module with eligibility checker and application guidance
- AR/VR Assisted First Aid Training: Use Augmented Reality (AR) in low-end smartphones to guide villagers step-by-step during emergencies (snakebite, fractures, bleeding, childbirth complications). Example: Point camera → overlay shows “tie cloth here to stop bleeding”. This makes the platform life-saving in remote emergencies, not just a consultation tool.
- Digital Twin of Community Health: Build a “Health Heatmap” of the village using anonymized data: Show hotspots for malaria, fevers, maternal complications. Help ASHAs and doctors predict outbreaks before they happen. This directly supports preventive healthcare instead of only reactive care.
- Voice-based Health Journaling: Users can record daily “voice logs” of health in their own dialect. The app automatically translates/summarizes for doctors. Preserves oral cultural style while feeding structured health data.
- Traditional Knowledge + AI Co-Pilot: Instead of a static repository, create an AI Co-Pilot that surfaces traditional remedies side-by-side with modern advice. Example: “For stomach pain: Doctor says ORS. Elders say neem bark tea (recorded by [Elder’s Name]).” This makes it culturally respectful, side-by-side knowledge instead of replacing one with another.
- Community Incentive Model: Introduce a token/reward system: Parents who complete child vaccination milestones earn Health Coins. Can be exchanged for small discounts at local shops/pharmacies. Incentivizes participation & ensures engagement.
- Health Education via Interactive “Katha” Stories: Instead of boring PDFs, use tribal folk storytelling style (audio/animation) to explain topics like vaccination, hygiene, maternal care. Local dialect voiceovers + drum/folk music → culturally immersive health education.
- AI-Powered Visual Symptom Scanner: Patients/ASHAs can take a photo of wounds, rashes, or swelling, and the app gives AI-powered preliminary guidance (safe vs. urgent referral). Especially useful for skin infections, injuries, maternal complications.
- Herbal Plant Identifier: Use camera to identify local plants and display their medicinal/traditional uses (crowdsourced + verified). Encourages community-driven preservation of biodiversity knowledge. Links to traditional knowledge module.
- AI-Powered Early Warning for Pregnancies: Predict risks by analyzing symptoms/journal entries + historical data. Send alerts to ASHA/doctors if a pregnancy is trending high-risk. Saves maternal lives → high emotional + practical impact.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Vibrant orange (#FF7043), reflecting energy and health.
- Background color: Soft peach (#FFE7D9), provides a comforting, neutral backdrop.
- Accent color: Sky blue (#64B5F6), complements the orange and conveys trust and clarity.
- Body font: 'PT Sans' a humanist sans-serif that is both modern and approachable.
- Headline font: 'Playfair', a modern serif similar to Didot, geometric, high contrast thin-thick lines, with an elegant, fashionable, high-end feel. Pair with 'PT Sans' for body text.
- Use simple, clear icons that are easily recognizable, even at a glance. Ensure icons are culturally relevant.
- Clean and intuitive layout, prioritizing large touch targets for voice-first interaction.
- Subtle animations and transitions to guide users and provide feedback without being distracting.
